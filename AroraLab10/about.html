Who we are
Philip Schechtman was born in Florida and later moved to Asheville in his sophmore year of highschool. Likewise, David Fine was born in Asheville and attended the same highschool as Philip. Here, their paths joined and their adventures started. When both attended UNC and then pledged to AEPi, they decided to partner up and find ways to earn some side cash. They began by cleaning their own fraternity house and have since moved to clean other collegiate fraternities. Philip Schechtman has been studying Computer Science and Econ, while David Schechtman has been studying Econ only. Both are dedicated and honest workers. Our mission
We are focused on bringing a more affortable, but effective cleaning service for customers who are unable to pay the full price for a commerical name brand cleaning service. We seek to build trust and positive relationships with our current and future customers.
